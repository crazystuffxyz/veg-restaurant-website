Our Beginning

Green Bites was seeded in March 2025 with a shared vision: to unite good food and conscious living. We made this restaurant because we couldn't find any sustainable restaurant with vegetarian options just like ours. By making this restaurant, we have stopped this struggle.

Motivated by a love for stainable cooking, we have made it our mission to revolutionize vegetarian cuisine. Vegetarian cuisine isn't just a matter of taking meat away or stopping the killing of animals; it's also a matter of sustainability, reducing global warming, and not making bland vegetables that taste like airplane food. Here at Green Bites, we have diverse dishes, each extremely flavorful so it doesn't feel like a burden to eat healthy.

Our Core Values

This is where at the heart of Green Bites we believe in quality and sustainability. Good food starts with high-quality ingredients, bought responsibly from other people who believe the same things as we do. Freshness and renewability matter to us, and you can taste it in every flavorful bite. We strive to use alternatives that are better for the earth in our business practices, minimize waste, and attempt to leave a light carbon footprint on this planet.

More Than Just Food

We didn't only build this restaurant to give people healthy alternatives; we made this restaurant a place where people can have fun making the world a better place. Where friendships are made, romances are born, and all individuals feel at home. Our atmosphere is casual, calm, and happy, so you can enjoy eating healthy food while interacting with others and minimizing carbon footprint.

Thank you so much for being a part of the Green Bites community. Together, we can try to make the world suitable for future generations to come, one bite at a time. Hungry and interested? Check out our menu and reserve!

Celebrating plant-based deliciousness with fresh, local ingredients and modern culinary flair. Join us for an unforgettable vegetarian dining experience in Vienna.
