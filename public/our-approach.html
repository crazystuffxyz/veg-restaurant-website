Our Approach - Freshness & Responsibility

Discover how our commitment to local sourcing, mindful preparation, and sustainability shapes every dish we serve.

Why is Green Bites Different?

At Green Bites, 'fresh' and 'sustainable' aren't buzzwords - they're what we're all about. We believe that being ethical in sourcing, preparation, and the way we touch the world makes not only better food, but a better meal experience - learn how our dedication makes every meal we serve better.

Sourcing

From Local Fields to Your Fork

We are dedicated to the celebration of Virginia's rich diversity. We create close relationships with local growers and producers who, along with us, are dedicated to sustainable and organic farming practices. That means buying foods season by season in their optimal flavor and nutritional quality.

By purchasing locally, we're not only ensuring the unparalled freshness, we're also leaving our money with our local producers and reducing the carbon footprint because of long-distance shipping. Seasonal menu allows you to always have the freshest of the best available in our area during the season.

We're glad to purchase locally from local producers for fresh greens like our spinach and arugula and sweet root vegetables like beets and carrots.

Preparation

Made with Love & Care

Magic happens in our kitchen. We are proud to let the quality of our ingredients speak for itself, and we do that by cooking almost everything from scratch every day. From our fresh-made dressing and sauces to our scratch-burgers veggie burger patties and fresh bread, we offer no shortcuts or pre-made ingredients.

Our chefs employ techniques which encourage natural flavors – roasting vegetables to reveal their sweetness, quick sautéing of greens so as to retain their life force, and gentle balancing of spices. We serve meals using whole foods that are unprocessed for a nourishing yet comforting meal.

Every meal is made to order with care to provide freshness and meeting special diet needs.

Responsibility

Responsible use of our planet

Reducing waste

We cut down food wastage ourselves by planning menus and stock planning. All of our green waste and edible food remnants are directed to composting to avoid redundant waste ending up in landfills.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Dine-ins and carry-outs both have packaging material that either is biodegradable, compostable, or made of recyclable material to minimize our dependence on single-use plastic.

Conscious Operations

We attempt to be frugal and reduce the consumption of resources with energy-efficient equipment, LED lighting, and low-water use procedures in the restaurant. We buy fair-trade coffee and other fair-trade products where possible.
